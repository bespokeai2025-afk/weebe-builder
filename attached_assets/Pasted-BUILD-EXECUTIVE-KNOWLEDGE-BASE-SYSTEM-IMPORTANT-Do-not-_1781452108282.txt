OBJECTIVE

Create a dedicated Executive Knowledge System for HiveMind, GrowthMind and SystemMind.

This knowledge system is separate from customer-facing agent knowledge bases.

Customer agent knowledge bases are used during calls.

Executive knowledge bases are used by the AI executives to make decisions, generate recommendations and create reports.
