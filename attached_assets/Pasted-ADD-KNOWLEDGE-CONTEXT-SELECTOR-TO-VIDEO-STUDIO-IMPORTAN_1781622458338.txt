ADD KNOWLEDGE CONTEXT SELECTOR TO VIDEO STUDIO

IMPORTANT

Do not remove existing Video Studio functionality.

Do not break current Business DNA integration.

Extend the system safely.

OBJECTIVE

Allow users to select which knowledge base and business context should be used when generating videos.

Current issue:

Video Studio assumes the current workspace business context.

Users need to create videos for:

* Their own company
* Client companies
* Test campaigns
* Multiple brands
* Different industries

====================================================

NEW SECTION

Add to Video Studio generation panel:

Knowledge Context

Dropdown:

Current Business (Default)

Specific Knowledge Base

Specific Client

Custom Campaign Context

No Knowledge Context

====================================================
