BUILD HEXMAIL DOMAIN WARMING + DELIVERABILITY SYSTEM

IMPORTANT

Do not create fake opens, fake replies, fake clicks, or artificial engagement.

Do not send emails to non-consenting recipients.

Do not build spam tools.

Do not bypass provider rules.

This system must be a legitimate email deliverability and domain reputation tool.

OBJECTIVE

Add a Domain Warming and Deliverability Centre directly inside the WeeBee Replit app.

This should sit under HexMail and GrowthMind because it supports outbound email marketing.

The purpose is to help users prepare new domains and mailboxes for safe email sending by:

* verifying DNS
* checking SPF, DKIM, DMARC
* monitoring bounces
* controlling daily send volume
* gradually increasing sending limits
* tracking reputation signals
* warning users before risky sending
