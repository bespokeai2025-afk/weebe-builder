RECOMMENDATION FORMAT

Each GrowthMind recommendation must contain:

- Clear title
- Campaign name and ID
- Category
- Priority: critical, high, medium or low
- Explanation of the issue
- Evidence and supporting metrics
- Previous-period comparison
- Expected benefit
- Recommended action
- Estimated impact where enough data exists
- Confidence level
- Date generated
- Current status
- Approval requirement
- Dismiss option
- “Review campaign” link

Example:

Title:
Reduce wasted spend from non-converting search terms

Evidence:
£428.20 spent across 14 search terms during the last 30 days with zero recorded conversions.

Recommended action:
Review the listed terms and add unsuitable queries as negative keywords.

Expected benefit:
Potential reduction in wasted spend while maintaining current conversion volume.

Do not generate vague recommendations such as “improve your campaign”. Every recommendation must be tied to retrieved campaign data.
