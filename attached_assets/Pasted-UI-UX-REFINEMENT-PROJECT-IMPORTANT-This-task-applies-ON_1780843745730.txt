GOAL

The dashboard currently feels too zoomed in.

Components are oversized.

Spacing is excessive.

Too few records fit on screen.

The product feels more like a consumer app than an enterprise SaaS platform.

Maintain the existing colour palette and branding.

Do NOT redesign the visual identity.

Focus only on sizing, density, spacing and information architecture.
