UPGRADE GROWTHMIND VIDEO STUDIO WITH FREE-FORM PROMPT TO VEO AD PIPELINE

IMPORTANT

Do not modify Retell, HyperStream, VoxStream, HiveMind, Builder, CRM, WhatsApp, or HexMail.

Extend GrowthMind Video Studio only.

OBJECTIVE

Allow users to paste a creative prompt into Video Studio and have GrowthMind convert it into a full 10/10 marketing video ad pipeline.

The user should be able to enter something like:

"Create a premium Meta video ad for our AI receptionist product targeting estate agents in the UK. Show missed calls turning into booked appointments. Make it cinematic, high trust, blue WeeBee branding, 20 seconds, strong CTA."

GrowthMind should generate:

1. Marketing angle
2. Target audience
3. Hook
4. Script
5. Storyboard
6. Scene-by-scene Veo prompt
7. Voiceover script
8. On-screen text
9. CTA
10. Final Veo generation request

====================================================

VEO AD QUALITY RULES

Before sending to Veo, GrowthMind must check:

Clear hook in first 3 seconds
Single target audience
Single clear offer
Clear visual story
Strong CTA
No vague claims
No overloaded text
Platform-specific aspect ratio
Brand colours included
Ad objective included

If missing, GrowthMind improves the prompt automatically.
