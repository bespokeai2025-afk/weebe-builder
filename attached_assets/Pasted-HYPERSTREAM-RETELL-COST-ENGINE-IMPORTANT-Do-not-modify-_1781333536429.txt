HYPERSTREAM & RETELL COST ENGINE

IMPORTANT

* Do not modify existing Retell functionality.
* Do not modify existing HyperStream functionality.
* This is an administrative feature only.
* Existing billing and usage tracking must remain unchanged.
* Generate implementation plan before coding.

OBJECTIVE

Create a platform-wide Cost Engine that calculates exact provider costs, exact cost per minute, exact profit per minute, and exact customer pricing.

The Cost Engine must support:

1. HyperStream
2. Retell
3. Future providers

The system must become the single source of truth for all pricing calculations.
